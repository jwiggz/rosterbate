Pre-Draft Pick Swaps
In snake drafts, teams only swap draft positions. No extra picks are exchanged, so the deal is just a clean slot-for-slot swap before the board opens.

That’s the whole deal in a snake draft: #1 for #10. No second swap, no compensation pick, just a straight draft-position trade before the room opens.
Weekly Powerups
Each week you can pull fresh powerups into your inventory, then fire one off to swing a matchup. Some are explosive, some are protection, and all of them make lineup decisions matter more.
Epic · Captain Mode
2x
Double one starter’s fantasy score for every game that week
Turn your best scorer into a weekly hammer. If your captain drops 38 fantasy points, you bank 76.
Rare · White Gloves
OUT
Injured starters copy your lowest active starter score that day
This is your injury shield. A dead starting slot can still scrape together points instead of giving you a zero.
Uncommon · Bench Boost
25%
Bench players with games add a quarter of their fantasy score
Stack your depth and squeeze extra production out of your bench instead of leaving those game days on the table.
Common · Sunday Surge
+25%
All starters score 25% more on Sunday
Perfect for tight matchups. Save it for the last day and make your whole lineup hit harder when the week is on the line.
Moneyball Modes
Lock in your best performances each week. Instead of scoring points from every game, you choose ONE game per position. Strategic timing and matchup selection become critical.
Step 1 · Choose Your Game
1
Pick one game per position each week to lock in
Your point guard has 4 games this week? You only lock in ONE. Choose wisely based on matchup, injury status, and schedule.
Step 2 · Lock Before Tipoff
🔒
Must lock before that game starts — no changes after
Once a game tips off, that lock is permanent. No swaps, no regrets. Make your call and live with it.
Step 3 · Only Locked Games Score
✓
Your locked game = your points for that position
All other games are ignored. If your locked player drops 40 fantasy points, you bank 40. If they go 2-12, you eat it.
