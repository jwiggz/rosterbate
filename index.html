Moneyball Modes
Lock in your best performances each week. Instead of scoring points from every game, you choose ONE game per position. Strategic timing and matchup selection become critical.
Step 1 · Choose Your Game
1
Pick one game per position each week to lock in
Your point guard has 4 games this week? You only lock in ONE. Choose wisely based on matchup, injury status, and schedule.
Step 2 · Lock Before Tipoff
🔒
Must lock before that game starts — no changes after
Once a game tips off, that lock is permanent. No swaps, no regrets. Make your call and live with it.
Step 3 · Only Locked Games Score
✓
Your locked game = your points for that position
All other games are ignored. If your locked player drops 40 fantasy points, you bank 40. If they go 2-12, you eat it.
