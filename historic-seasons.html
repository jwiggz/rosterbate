Mode Stack
What This Lane Becomes
The goal is not one nostalgia gimmick. The goal is a reusable archive layer where every season can support three different products at once.
Play The Real Season Launch the era with historical rosters intact and let the daily reveal system make the season feel lived-in.
Draft The Era Open the entire player pool from that year and build an alternate-history league around the best names of the season.
Reimagined Season Future direction: sim the season into a new branch before the user even drafts, so the same era can be replayed in a different reality.
Audit Mindset
Pack Truth Over Hype
Historical mode only works if users trust it. This browser keeps the pack health visible: real-stat coverage, zero-game roster-only cases, and removed invalid players stay front-and-center so future eras can be audited quickly.
